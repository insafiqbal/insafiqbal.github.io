Why Sri Lanka ?

Situated in the edge of Sri Lanka is the beauty of the Arugam Bay. Our branch in Sri Lanka Offers a wide range of cultures to experience on your stay here. Umluj

Why Saudi Arabia ?

Located on the edge of Saudi Arabia in a place called Umluj is our highest rated branch out of all. Theres a reason why people love Saudi Arabia the most. Hulhumale
Maldives

Why maldives ?

Nestled on its own in the middle of the ocean is the island called Hulhumale, where beauty comes to reality. Words cannot express the beauty that Hulhumale offers you. The Laviant Bay will be the first ever Luxury Beach hotel to be opened in the island of Hulhumale. The word culture is not enough to describe the experience we can have in hulhumale. The laviant Bay offers special services here to make your stay stressfree. With our own private beaches and own ushers to help you and comfort you.
